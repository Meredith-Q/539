Mahjong competition

2024.6.14

Pandas

The Year-End Ranking League for Daily Mahjong Intelligence Competition is here! The recent Crazy Thursdays will be replaced by Saturday Daily Mahjong Intelligence Competitions. Four qualifying rounds will be held starting November 8th, with one round every other weekend. Tentatively scheduled for Saturdays: November 8th, November 22nd, December 6th, and December 20th. The top player from each qualifying round advances to represent their store in the annual championship competition. The ultimate champion of the Japan Mahjong Intelligence League will receive a cash prize of 2,500 yuan. Players may visit the Japan Mahjong Intelligence Competition homepage to register for their desired qualifying round. Only the champion may not participate in subsequent rounds; registration remains open for all other rounds. Please register in advance to secure your spot.
